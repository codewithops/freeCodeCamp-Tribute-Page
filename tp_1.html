Whitney Elizabeth Houston

1963-2012

Singer and Actress


1963

Whitney Elizabeth Houston was born on August 9, 1963 in Newark, New Jersey.

1981

As a teenager, Houston's beauty was quickly recognized. She began to model and appeared on the cover of Seventeen Magazine in November of that year.

1983

At just the age of 19, Whitney met Clive Davis and this started their long professional relationship. Davis signed Houston and they spent 2 years working on her debut album.

1985

Her debut album, called Whitney Houston was released and instantly starrocked her career.

Her singles "Saving All My Love" and "How Will I Know" where at the top of the charts for 14 non-consective weeks.

1986

She won her first Grammy for "Saving All My Love for You"

1987

Whitney Houston released her second album called “Whitney”. This album went platinum many times over and she also won a Grammy for "I Wanna Dance With Somebody (Who Loves Me).

1989

Whitney created The Whitney Houston Foundation for Children. This non-profit organization raised money for children in need, specifically those experiencing homelessness, cancer, AIDS, and self-empowerment issues.

1992

Whitney married singer, Bobby Brown.

Houston starred in The Bodyguard, with Kevin Costner. This movie was wildy popular and she earned respect as an actress. For the soundtrack of the movie, Houston covered Dolly Parton’s “I Will Always Love You” and it became her biggest hit.

After her success in The Body Guard, Houston also starred in two additional movies in the 1990s, Waiting to Exhale and The Preacher’s Wife. Similar to The Body Guard, she was featured in the popular soundtracks.

1998

Houston released her album “My Love Is Your Love” and also earned a Grammy for her single, “It’s Not Right But It’s Okay”.

2002

Houston released her fifth studio album called Just Whitney.

2006

Houston was named the most-awarded female artist of all time by Guinness World Records

2009

Houston released a new album called “I Look To You”. This album was loved by fans and did well on the album charts.

2012

Houston began working on the musical film Sparkle. She also had other work projects planned for this year. Sadly, she didn’t have the opportunity to pursue those plans.

Houston died on February 11, 2012, in Los Angeles. She was staying at the Beverly Hilton hotel where a Grammy party was being held by Clive Davis. The official cause of death as accidental drowning. The effects of heart disease and cocaine found in her systems were contributing factors also.

2020

Whitney Houston was inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame.
